About the Writer

A personal archive of faith, femininity, grief, desire, and growth.

I write to make sense of the tender, dramatic, holy, and complicated pieces of becoming. My work explores girlhood, longing, relationships, faith, identity, healing, and the quiet moments that shape who we are.

This portfolio is a living room for my words: polished pieces, raw letters, journal-like essays, and creative fragments that tell the truth with beauty.

Why I Write

Because feelings deserve a body. Because memory deserves language. Because becoming is easier when it has a witness.

Writing Philosophy

Be honest, be beautiful, be brave. Let softness be powerful. Let faith and desire sit on the same page.

Favorite Quotes
